Introduction

This project, ChatGPT Efficiency Evaluation, focuses on assessing the performance of ChatGPT by comparing its responses to a set of predefined database answers. The aim is to evaluate the accuracy and adaptability of the model when faced with diverse questions and unique challenges.

The evaluation process involves two approaches. First, a lexical comparison measures the textual similarity between ChatGPT's responses and the database answers. Second, ChatGPT itself is used as a reviewer, determining which response—its own or the database’s—is more accurate and relevant to the given question.

Additionally, the project incorporates visualizations to summarize the analysis. Graphs based on 20 randomized samples highlight patterns and trends, offering insights into ChatGPT's performance across different datasets.

This initiative not only demonstrates the capabilities of ChatGPT but also showcases a structured approach to evaluating and visualizing AI performance.
